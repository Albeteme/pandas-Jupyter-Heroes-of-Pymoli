Observation of trends
1) Charter Schools are in the top 5 schools with a passing rate >95% while the less performing are District schools with less than 82%.
2) The budget per student seems to be in a lower range for Charter schools (578$-638$) compared to the District schools (637$-655$). 
3) The more money is spent per student, the lower passing grade seem to be. The schools that spend less money present the best results.
